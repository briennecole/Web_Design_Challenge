How does latitude affect weather patterns?

Latitude and Weather

This site displays the affect global latitude has on four weather conditions (Maximum Temperature, Humidity, Level of Cloudiness and Wind Speed). Calculated externally using python and matplotlib, we examined the historical weather data from over 500 cities across the globe of varying distance from the equator.
